QUARANTINE

A NEW GAME MODE IS COMING

Don't miss a beat, QUARANTINE is coming in Rainbow Six Siege. Experience first hand as the Rainbow Operators take on a dangerous bioterrorist group. New Operators specialized in rescue missions join the Rainbow Team. With them, expect a new massive and action-filled map into the game.

GOYO
Specialist César “Goyo” Ruiz Hernández surprised me. Young though he may be, his innate comprehension of human behaviors makes him a formidable opponent.
AMARU
Specialist Azucena Rocío “Amaru” Quispe is cautious, that much is clear. But behind that caution lies a warmth and versatility that I’ve rarely seen before.

NEW OPERATORS

For the fifth year of Rainbow 7 Siege, give some space to our two new operators as they break into this new game mode with their unique abilities. Two operators, Amaru and Goyo, were recruited from Peru and Mexico, bringing with them innovation and change to Rainbow Seven Siege.

NEW MAP: BARTLETT UNIVERSITY
Team Rainbow has been called to assist local law enforcement and regain control of Bartlett University in Cambridge, Massachusetts. This map offers an asymmetric layout with breathing room between hot zones. Navigate the facilities' barricades and blast through the brick walls that have protected the leaders of tomorrow.
